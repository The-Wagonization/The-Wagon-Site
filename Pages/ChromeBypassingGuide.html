Finding Information The 3 things exploits most likely may need are your board name, kernel version, &/or version. Note you may need other things, such as an external storage device.
Board Name In order to find out your board name, go to chrome://version. It will be behind "stable-channel".
Kernver In order to find out your Kernver, enter Recovery Mode (Esc+Refresh+Power) & then press Tab, or you can visit chrome://system. Only the last digit matters.
Keyrolled If you're keyrolled you can't boot shims, so just try & boot a shim to check.
OS Version In order to find your OS version, you can do a few things things. The easiest & most precise method is to go to chrome://version or go to "About Chrome OS" in Settings. The second & less precise method is to eyeball it.
Miscellaneous Extension IDs: found in the address bar on either the extension's details or webstore page.
Extension lists: found in either the top-right corner/3 dots or chrome://extensions (not to be confused with the page named the Extension List).
Inspect Element: either right-click or click under the 3 dots. Shortcut is Ctrl+Shift+I.
